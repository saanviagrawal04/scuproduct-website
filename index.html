Our Story

Building the next generation of product leaders

Why a Product Management Club was needed

As students passionate about product management, Saanvi Agrawal and Vaishnavi Tatipamula found themselves navigating a field that was exciting, but difficult to break into. We both realized that while interest in PM was growing on campus, resources and guidance were limited.

We constantly ran into the same challenges:

  • No clear roadmap for how to prepare
  • Little visibility into real-world PM expectations
  • Few chances to connect with mentors or recruiters

We created ProductSpace to change that, a place where any student, from any background, could explore PM through community, events, and hands-on experiences.

Our Mission

ProductSpace Product Management Club (PSPMC) will aim to:

  • Foster community among students with an interest in Product and Project Management on Santa Clara University's campus through guest speakers, workshops such as acing the product interview and participating in product case competitions, and real world experience through clients.
  • Educate the Santa Clara campus about opportunities in the Product and Project space.
  • Establish a greater and more inclusive sense of community by collaborating with other student organizations and professionals in the field.
